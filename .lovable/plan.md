

# Noah Fearnley — Official Celebrity Website

## Overview
A professional, modern celebrity website inspired by paulmccartney.com and sylvesterstallone.com. Clean, minimalistic design with a black/white/gray palette accented with blue. Text-focused with subtle animations, bold headings, and prominent CTAs driving fan club sign-ups.

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Header / Navigation
- Fixed top bar with "Noah Fearnley" in an elegant serif font as the logo
- Menu: Home, About, Filmography, News, Gallery, Events, Fan Club, Contact
- Social media icons (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok) with placeholder links
- Mobile-responsive hamburger menu
- Subtle scroll effect (background darkens on scroll)

### 2. Home Page
- **Hero Section**: Full-width background image placeholder with overlay text — "Welcome to the Official Website of Noah Fearnley" and subtitle "Rising Actor | From Vertical Dramas to Hollywood Screens"
- **Intro Paragraph**: Brief welcoming text about Noah
- **CTA Button**: "Join the Exclusive Fan Club" — prominent, blue-accented
- **Promo Blocks** (card-style):
  - "Mercy" (2026) — synopsis, image placeholder, "Learn More" button
  - Vertical Dramas — carousel of thumbnail cards with "Watch Highlights" button
- **Latest News** preview section (2-3 cards linking to News page)

### 3. About Page
- **Interactive Timeline**: Vertical timeline with milestones:
  - 1999: Born in Orange, CT
  - 2015-2016: Knee injury, pivot to modeling & acting
  - Training at Tampa's Actors School, Woolson, Lesly Kahn
  - 2023: ReelShort career begins
  - 2024: Debut in "Morgan's Secret Admirer"
  - 2025-2026: Lifetime films, "Mercy," "Love Story"
- **Detailed Bio Section**: Education, personal life (marriage to Aalayah, 1968 Mustang restoration, fitness), career philosophy
- **Inspirational Quote Block**: "From football fields to film sets, every setback is a setup for a comeback."

### 4. Filmography Page
- **Tabbed Layout** with three tabs:
  - **Vertical Dramas**: Cards for 10+ titles (Escorting the Heiress, Fade Till the End, Virgin's Bucket List, Gold Hearts of Hot Rod County, etc.) with role names, platform, short descriptions, image placeholders
  - **TV & Films**: Cards for Black Girl Missing, Mother's Deadly Son, Mercy, Love Story with synopses, cast, release dates
  - **Modeling**: Brief section on early Miami/LA agency work
- Each card has an image placeholder and expandable details

### 5. News Page
- Blog-style article list with date, headline, excerpt, and "Read More" links
- Placeholder articles: "Noah Joins Cast of 'Mercy' with Chris Pratt," "Upcoming Fan Events," etc.
- Clean, readable layout with pagination

### 6. Gallery Page
- Grid layout with lightbox functionality for viewing full-size images
- Category filters: "On Set," "Personal," "Events"
- Placeholder images with elegant hover effects

### 7. Events Page
- List/card layout of upcoming appearances
- Each event card: title, date, location, description, "Get Tickets" button
- Placeholder entries (VIP Meet-and-Greet at LA Film Festival, etc.)

### 8. Fan Club Page
- Benefits section with styled bullet points highlighting:
  - Members-only content & exclusive access
  - Meet-and-greet events & private shows
  - VIP treatment (front-row seats, backstage passes)
  - Auctions & giveaways (signed scripts, props, memorabilia)
- **Sign-up form**: Name, email, password fields with validation
- Styled membership tiers visual (even if single tier for now)

### 9. Contact Page
- Contact form (name, email, subject, message) with validation
- Management/press email placeholder
- Social media links

### 10. Footer
- Copyright notice, Privacy Policy & Terms links
- Social media icons
- Mailing list sign-up (separate from fan club) with email input + subscribe button

---

## Design System
- **Colors**: Black, white, grays with blue accent (#3B82F6 range) evoking Noah's blue eyes
- **Typography**: Elegant serif for headings, clean sans-serif for body text
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-in on scroll, smooth hover transitions on buttons and cards
- **Layout**: Centered, max-width container, generous whitespace, minimalistic

## Technical Notes
- include backend for the contents and connect the social media links to his actual social media accounts
